BECOME A FRIEND OF RuCTF

RuCTF is the largest information security event in the Ural region and one of the largest throughout Russia.


Our main goals are to develop the professional information security community, share knowledge and help implement awesome and innovative ideas. We always welcome cooperation offers and opportunities to support the company's initiative.
