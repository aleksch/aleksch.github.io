ORGANIZERS

We are the Hackerdom team. Over the past 15 years we have held more than 40 computer security competitions and trained about 12,000 students.


It all started in the fall of 2005 at the Mathematics and Mechanics Department of Ural State University. In 2008 RuCTF was born. Apart from that, every year there are 2 other no less cool events - RuCTFE and QCTF.


Our main partners are Ural Federal University and Association of Information Security Service Chiefs (ARSIB).
